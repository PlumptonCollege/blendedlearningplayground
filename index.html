Hot questions (Active Inspire Interactive WhiteBoards Activity)

Hot questions is a useful way to help answer any questions that pupils may have. This task allows for further understanding and remembering through student interaction.


What you need

路         Interactive Whiteboard.

路         ActivInspire Software.

What you do

At the end of a lecture or during the end of any topics, ask your pupils to have a think about any questions they may have. Next, ask them to come to the whiteboard and write their questions on the board. Once they have, the teacher can discuss the questions with the class. The questions can then be savedon the board, this allows for recapping or simply to show the students progress by enquiring their knowledge learnt

Activity variables

路         Get the students to work in groups of three or more and come up with one question between the group.

路         Use the questions on the whiteboard to ask the students to answer rather than the teacher.
